# **4-Day Multi-Frequency Workout Routine**

:::info[Info]
Questa routine è un aggiornamento della [**Routine 02**](./02_routine-02.mdx), rivista e trasformata in allenamento multi-frequenza, pensato per 4 giorni.
:::

## 📌 Day 1: Shoulders, Biceps, Legs

### Shoulder Exercises

**1️⃣ Lateral Raise, Dumbbell** *(Alzate laterali con manubri)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 10
- Weight: 10kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**2️⃣ Overhead Shoulder Press, Dumbbell** *(Lento in avanti con manubri)* - Option B

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 10
- Weight: 10-15kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

### Bicep Exercise

**1️⃣ Bayesian Curl, Cable** *(Bayesian Curl con cavo)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 10
- Weight: 14kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**2️⃣ Preacher Curl, Cable, EZ Grip** *(Curl su panca Scott con presa EZ ai cavi)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 10
- Weight: 20-24kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

### Leg Exercises

**1️⃣ Super Hack Squat, Machine** *(Super Hack Squat alla macchina)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 10
- Weight: 20kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**2️⃣ Hip Thrust, Machine** *(Hip thrust, macchina)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 10
- Weight: 15kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**3️⃣ Hip Abduction, Machine** *(Abduzione delle anche alla macchina)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 10
- Weight: 40kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**4️⃣ Lying Leg Curl, Machine** *(Leg curl sdraiato alla macchina)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 12
- Weight: 20-25kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

### Calf Exercises

**1️⃣ Calf Press, Machine** *(Pressa per i polpacci alla macchina)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 20
- Weight: 30-40kg
- 🕒 1 min 15 sec

**2️⃣ Seated Calf Raise** *(Calf raise seduto)*

- Sets: 5
- Reps: 20
- Weight: 30-40kg
- 🕒 1 min 15 sec

## 📌 Day 2: Chest, Back, Triceps

### Chest Exercises

**1️⃣ Bench Press, Barbell** *(Panca piana con bilanciere)*

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 8-10
- Weight: 40-50kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**2️⃣ Incline Bench Press, Dumbbell** *(Panca inclinata con manubri)*

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 8-10
- Weight: 12.5-14kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**3️⃣ Incline Chest Fly, Dumbbell** *(Aperture su panca inclinata con manubri)*

- Sets: 3
- Reps: 8-10
- Weight: 10-12.5kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

### Back

**1️⃣ Lat Pulldown, Cable** *(Lat machine con cavo)*

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 10-12
- Weight: 35-45kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**2️⃣ Seated Row, Machine** *(Rematore seduto alla macchina)*

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 10-12
- Weight: 40-50kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**3️⃣ Reverse Grip Lat Pulldown, Cable** *(Lat machine con presa inversa ai cavi)*

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 10-12
- Weight: 40-45kg
- 🕒 1 min 15 sec

### Triceps Exercises

**1️⃣ Skullcrusher, Barbell** *(French press con bilanciere)*

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 8-10
- Weight: 25kg (considering a barbell of 10kg)
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec

**2️⃣ Triceps Extension, Dumbbell** *(Estensioni per tricipiti con manubrio)*

- Sets: 3
- Reps: 6-9
- Weight: 14kg
- 🕒 1 min

**3️⃣ Triceps Rope Pushdown, Cable** *(Spinte verso il basso con corda ai cavi per tricipiti)*

- Sets: 4
- Reps: 10-12
- Weight: 10-15kg
- 🕒 1 min 30 sec
